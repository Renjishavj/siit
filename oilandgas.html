Course Modules Overview

The Oil & Gas Engineering program provides comprehensive training covering production operations, instrumentation, mechanical systems, electrical engineering, and industrial safety to prepare students for real-world energy sector careers.

Oil & Gas Production

Introduction to oil & gas industry operations including drilling, wellhead equipment, pumping systems, refining processes, and petroleum production technologies.

Process Control & Instrumentation

Study of industrial measurement systems, process variables, PLC, SCADA, DCS control systems, calibration techniques, and automation used in modern plants.

Mechanical Engineering

Maintenance and operation of pumps, compressors, heat exchangers, piping systems, HVAC equipment, and mechanical testing used in oil & gas facilities.

Electrical Engineering

Fundamentals of electrical systems including power generation, industrial wiring, transformers, UPS systems, substations, and automation controls.

Fire & Industrial Safety

Industrial safety practices, hazard analysis, PPE usage, fire prevention methods, emergency response planning, and safety standards followed in oil & gas industries.

Eligibility

  • Basic qualification to join the course is +2.
  • Suitable for B.Tech / BE / ITI / Diploma (Mechanical, Electrical, Electronics, Instrumentation).
  • Candidates with +2 or any graduation background can apply. Our training ensures complete technical understanding and industry readiness.

Placement Opportunities

Successful completion of this course opens doors to various career opportunities worldwide in rigs, power generation, manufacturing, shipyards, oil refineries, LNG, organic chemicals, carbon & chemical industries.

Students can achieve the following designations across multiple industrial sectors:

Drilling Engineer | Process Technician | Instrument Technician | Industrial Electrician | Fire & Safety Officer | Rigger | Production Engineer | QC Inspector | QC Engineer | Materials Engineer | Research & Development Engineer
